Medical Forms

Parts A and B are required for all Scouts and adults participating in any Scouting events. This is the one the adult or the Scout’s parents fill out. Parts A and B cover things like contact information, insurance coverage, health history, hold harmless release, photo release, and adult or parent signature.


Part C is the physical form. You need a physician’s signature on the medical form. Part C is required for Scouts and adults if any of the following apply:

  • The event will last more than 72 hours
  • The event is a high adventure base event
  • The event is strenuous and demanding

    About

    Additional Information

    Welcome to Troop 706, Glyndon, MD. We are sponsored by Sacred Heart Church. We accept boys of all faiths. Our goal is to help transition boys into young men and ultimately good adults. We have monthly camping trips plus summer camp. Meetings are held every Thursday @ 6:30pm in the West Room. Boy Scouts are open to boys between the ages of 10½ and 18. Prior Cub Scout membership is NOT REQUIRED. Please note: If Baltimore County Schools are closed or afternoon activities cancelled, Scout activities at Sacred Heart Glyndon are cancelled. The Troop may move the meeting to Christ The King.
